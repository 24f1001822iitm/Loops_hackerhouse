II. Alex's Tools

Six things she can do on a call.

Alex's intelligence is the combination of ElevenLabs voice synthesis and six small, specific tools — each a webhook that runs against the insurer's own backend and returns in under 500 milliseconds. Miss that window and she can't sound human.

lookup_claim

Find the claim.

Looks up an existing claim by number; returns status, adjuster, and documents on file.

typically 312ms claim_id: CLM-2026-000456

check_policy

Check the coverage.

Pulls a policy by number; returns what it covers and its deductible, line by line.

typically 188ms policy_number: POL-2024-001234

check_documents

See what's missing.

Compares the required documents against what's already been received for a given claim.

typically 204ms claim_id: CLM-2026-000456

file_claim

File a new one.

Creates a new claim with type, date, and description; returns a fresh claim number on the line.

typically 412ms claim_type: collision

escalate_to_human

Hand off when stuck.

Writes an escalation with reason and priority; routes the caller to a supervisor queue.

typically 156ms priority: urgent

schedule_callback

Call back later.

Parses a natural-language time ("tomorrow afternoon") and books a callback on the calendar.

typically 244ms preferred_time: "tomorrow 2pm"

All six hit the same Fastify backend, on Railway, backed by Supabase. Add your own, and Alex learns them on deploy.
